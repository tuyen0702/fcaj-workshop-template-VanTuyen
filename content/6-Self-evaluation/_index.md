During my internship at **Amazon Web Services Vietnam Company Limited** from **17/04/2026** to **30/07/2026**, I had the opportunity to participate in the **First Cloud AI Journey** program. Through this internship, I was able to learn, practice, and apply knowledge related to cloud computing and AWS services.

Throughout the internship, most of the learning process was carried out through self-study, including watching lesson videos, reading technical documentation, and completing selected hands-on labs on AWS. The learning content covered fundamental AWS services such as **Amazon VPC, Amazon EC2, Amazon S3, Amazon CloudFront, IAM, Amazon RDS, Amazon DynamoDB, Amazon CloudWatch, AWS Lambda, Amazon API Gateway, Amazon EventBridge, AWS Step Functions, and Amazon Bedrock**.

In addition, I worked with my team to research, design, and implement the project **AI AWS Architecture Reviewer**. This system supports the analysis of AWS architecture diagrams using AI, estimates monthly costs, and generates architecture review reports. Through this project, I gained more practical experience in combining multiple AWS services to build a complete cloud-based system.

The internship environment was flexible and comfortable. Students mainly studied online and could register to work at the office or participate in events when needed. This helped me become more proactive in managing my learning schedule, following the internship progress, and completing assigned tasks. I also had the chance to join events where I could listen to industry professionals from different companies share their real-world experience, which helped me better understand the cloud industry and career expectations.

Since the program had a large number of participants, with more than 700 students, connecting with all other interns was quite challenging. Communication mainly took place through online messages. However, the support team and company members were still helpful in answering students’ questions, especially when we encountered difficulties during learning, practice, or project implementation. Working in a group also allowed me to connect with a smaller group of students, exchange ideas, divide tasks, and support each other during the project.

In terms of technical skills, I believe I made clear progress in understanding and using AWS services. From having only basic theoretical knowledge, I gradually learned how cloud services can be combined to build a complete system. During the group project, I participated in requirement analysis, architecture design, workflow research, project implementation, and report writing. As a result, I improved my self-learning ability, system analysis skills, documentation skills, teamwork skills, and problem-solving ability.

In terms of working attitude, I always tried to complete the assigned learning content and project tasks. Since the internship was mostly based on self-study and online communication, I had to be more independent in managing my time, tracking progress, and researching solutions. However, I also realized that I still need to improve my discipline, communication skills, and problem-solving mindset when dealing with complex technical issues.

### General Reflection

Overall, the internship helped me gain valuable knowledge and practical experience in cloud computing. I not only learned about AWS services in theory but also had the opportunity to apply them to a real project. Since the program mainly required self-study, I was able to improve my independence, research ability, and problem-solving skills.

Besides the achievements, I also recognized some limitations. At some points, I was not very active in communicating with students outside my team because most activities were online and the number of participants was large. In addition, when facing technical errors or unfamiliar problems, I sometimes spent a lot of time identifying the root cause and choosing the most suitable solution.

However, through this internship, I learned how to approach problems step by step, search for official documentation, ask questions when support was needed, and understand how teamwork works in a technology project. These experiences are valuable for my future study and career development.

### Areas for Improvement

* Improve discipline in time management, learning progress tracking, and completing tasks according to plan.
* Improve problem-solving thinking, especially in analyzing technical errors and selecting suitable solutions.
* Be more proactive in communication, work discussion, and asking questions when facing difficulties.
* Improve the ability to present ideas, report progress, and explain technical solutions clearly.
* Continue learning more deeply about AWS, especially in security, monitoring, serverless, cost optimization, and system architecture.
