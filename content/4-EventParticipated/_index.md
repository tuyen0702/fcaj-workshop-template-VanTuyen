# Events Participated

During my internship, I had the opportunity to attend technology events organized by the AWS community. These events provided practical experiences that helped me stay updated with emerging technologies, explore solutions used in enterprise environments, and learn from experienced speakers in the industry.

Through these events, I expanded my knowledge of AWS, Generative AI, and system architecture. I also improved my ability to listen, summarize technical information, connect with the community, and apply what I learned to real-world projects.

## [Event 1 – AWS Vietnam Community Day – May 2026](4.1-Event1/)

&emsp;**Event name:** AWS Vietnam Community Day – May 2026

&emsp;**Date:** May 23, 2026

&emsp;**Location:** 26th Floor, Bitexco Financial Tower, Ho Chi Minh City

&emsp;**Organizer:** FCAJ – First Cloud AI Journey

&emsp;**Attendance format:** In person

&emsp;**Role:** Attendee

&emsp;**Main content:** The event included multiple sessions on context engineering, Amazon Quick Suite, Amazon CloudFront, the non-deterministic behavior of large language models, enterprise-grade multi-agent architecture, and experiences from building AI products during a hackathon.

&emsp;**Results and value gained:** I gained a clearer understanding of how to provide effective context for AI, design multi-agent systems, implement guardrails and output validation, and use CloudFront to improve application performance, security, and reliability. These lessons can be directly applied to the AI AWS Architecture Reviewer project.
